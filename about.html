About ViAGO

30 years helping operations run better. The agent era is why it matters now.

ViAGO is a specialist consultancy built on the Theory of Constraints. We help freight forwarders and customs brokers get more from their CargoWise investment, run smoother operations, and prepare their people for whatever comes next.

What We Do

We don't teach people to do their job better. We change how they think about the job.

We create whole-system alignment, improve inter-team communication, and give everyone a common language for decision making and problem solving that controls risk and supports growth.

We deliver this by visualising problems and solutions in cause and effect. Simplifying seemingly complex business challenges, and sorting the facts from the noise. That is how you ensure your people are, at any point, making the right decisions and working on the things that will deliver the greatest possible return.

For over 30 years, we have trained business owners, managers, leaders, and operational staff in logical thinking processes and leadership skills across almost every industry imaginable. Combining training experience with a project approach, we implement reliable change in teams and people.

Black Belt in Thinking

The thinking-skills programme behind everything we do.

BBIT has been deployed across some of the largest freight operations in the world. It is not a standalone personal development course. It is the prerequisite for the rest of the operation working as designed.
