The challenge

Like most freight forwarders, Mainfreight had always sought to improve operational efficiency — but often fell into making process changes reactively, after a shipment missed or a service failure that shouldn't have happened.

The goal was to see possible problems before they escalated, complete jobs in the right order, share workloads evenly, and surface bottlenecks before they hit customers.

The problem.

Freight forwarding operations are inherently variable — vessel delays, documentation exceptions, customs holds. The question isn't whether problems will occur. It's whether you see them in time to do something about them.

Mainfreight's teams were skilled and experienced, but the work was invisible. There was no single view of what was in progress, what was at risk, or where the queue was building. Workloads weren't balanced across the team. The signal that something was going wrong was usually a customer complaint.

The existing approach to improvement was reactive: fix the process after the failure. What was needed was a system that made the flow visible before failures happened.

The work.

ViAGO implemented the CargoWise One Productivity Suite across the Mainfreight Air & Ocean Australia operation — configuring workflow visualisation across the shipment lifecycle.

The implementation was done with the teams, not at them. Mainfreight staff were engaged in planning and implementation from the start, not handed a finished system. That mattered: the people who understand where the problems actually occur are the operators, not the consultants.

The configuration mapped each critical milestone in the sea and air import and export workflow, created visibility of work in progress, and surfaced the constraint — which jobs were at risk, which queues were building, and which teams were under or overloaded.

What changed.

"Like most freight forwarders, Mainfreight has always sought out ways to improve the efficiency of our operations."

Robert Cotter, Business Solutions and Process Manager, Mainfreight Air & Ocean Australia

  • All five key internal KPIs improved substantially, correlating directly with the point at which the productivity tools were fully implemented.
  • Workloads became visible and balanced across the team.
  • Problems surfaced before they reached customers, not after.
  • An unexpected benefit: the daily productivity stand-ups that came with the implementation identified and developed the next generation of team leaders.
