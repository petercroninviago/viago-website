Training

Per Person

Black Belt in Thinking

Every stage of Automate, Delegate, Accelerate makes higher demands on human judgement. Automating requires people who understand their own workflows. Delegating requires people who know when an agent has got it wrong and why. Managing escalations under pressure requires rapid cause-and-effect reasoning and the ability to decide fast without second-guessing.

These are trainable skills. BBIT is that training — the same thinking discipline that PAVE is built on, applied to the humans who run the operation. Not a standalone personal development course. The prerequisite for the rest of the operation working as designed.

PAVE Certification

Three levels, each building on the last. PCO gives operators the foundation — they know which job to work on next and why. PCS gives team leaders the systems view, managing flow across the whole operation, not just individual jobs. PCP gives operations managers the tools to design, tune and maintain the PAVE system as the business changes.
