Automate the workflow.

Configure CargoWise milestones, tasks, and triggers before agents arrive. Without this foundation, agents can't execute — humans become the handoff between systems.

02
Delegate to agents.

As AI Agents come online, tasks can be delegated to them first to handle the predictable and business-as-usual work, while escalation exceptions are handled by humans.

03
Accelerate the operator.

PAVE catches everything in one place — agent reviews, escalations, and direct human work. The operator manages the whole operation from a single prioritised surface.
