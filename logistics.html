How It Works

Automate. Delegate. Accelerate. All three happen inside CargoWise One.

Each stage is a configuration problem before it's a training problem. ViAGO does both.

Step 1

Automate

Configure milestones, tasks, and triggers in CargoWise so agents have a clean system to work with. Messy configuration means agents create new problems faster than they solve old ones.

Step 2

Delegate

Commercial Invoice Parsing, ComplianceWise, and Classification Assistant handle the document processing and compliance checks. Humans touch the shipment only at statutory checkpoints.

Step 3

Accelerate

PAVE surfaces the work that needs human attention — scheduled reviews and escalations — and buffers your team against surge. When volumes spike, operators see what matters first.

PAVE

The workflow visualisation tool built into CargoWise. We wrote its certification.

PAVE makes work visible. It surfaces the constraint, smooths the flow, and tells your operators what to work on next — not what they feel like working on.

ViAGO was engaged by WiseTech Global to write the PAVE certification programme, now available in WiseTech Academy. We don't just implement PAVE. We define what good looks like.

Black Belt in Thinking

These are trainable skills. BBIT is that training — the same thinking discipline PAVE itself is built on.
