The Training Trap

Your operators are being trained for the work that's about to disappear.

Lodge the entry. Check the invoice. Update the milestone. Clear the exception queue. The operators who are "good at CargoWise" today are good at exactly the tasks that won't exist in 24 months.

The skill they'll actually need is different: rapid cause-and-effect reasoning under pressure, spotting the hidden assumption in a stuck situation, resolving conflicts without compromising. The Black Belt in Thinking programme, available through WiseTech Academy, was built for exactly this gap. But most operators haven't done it, and most firms haven't prioritised it. That's a problem with a closing window.

The Shift

When agents take the transactional work, escalations become the job.

Agent-driven operations don't eliminate human work. They concentrate it. The predictable tasks go to agents. What's left is the stuff that doesn't fit a pattern: ambiguous escalations, novel situations, trade-offs, judgement calls.

That's not a nice-to-have skill. It's the entire remaining value of a human in the operation. When an agent escalates, the operator needs to understand what's happened, what's at stake, and what to do, quickly and under pressure. Without a formal thinking framework, people default to pattern-matching on whatever looks familiar. That's exactly when bad decisions happen at speed.

The System

PAVE makes the work visible. ARS makes the response disciplined.

PAVE is CargoWise's native workflow visualisation engine, built on Drum-Buffer-Rope. It surfaces work-in-progress, identifies the constraint, and makes flow predictable. ViAGO co-developed it with CargoWise, and authored the certification that defines what "good" looks like.

But dashboards alone don't change anything. ARS, our Alternate Response System, is the disciplined protocol that turns PAVE's signals into action. Three states: Watch when things are green. Plan when things are calm, pre-committing to what the red-zone response will be. Act when the signal goes red, executing the pre-committed response immediately. No re-litigating the decision under pressure. Decide in the cold, act in the heat.
